Our Research

We address challenges at the intersection of nanomedicine, biomaterials, and healthcare technologies.

Nanoparticle Design and Delivery

We design advanced lipid and polymeric nanoparticles for efficient delivery of RNA therapeutics, targeting diseases such as cancer, obesity, and asthma.

Biomaterials for Regenerative Medicine

Our team develops hydrogels and scaffold systems to support tissue engineering and controlled drug release, with a focus on structure-function relationships.

Surface Engineering and Immune Modulation

We study how nanoparticle and biomaterial surface properties affect interactions with cells, tissues, and the immune system, aiming for safer and more effective therapies.
